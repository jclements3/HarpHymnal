192 Amazing Grace — Single-Tactic Audition

G major · 3/4 · SATB baseline + one-tactic-at-a-time
How to listen. Every demo plays the full 16-bar hymn as a plain SATB chorale on orchestral harp, with ONE tactic audibly applied to a single spotlight bar. The spotlight is bar 5 by default (pure tonic chord after the opening phrase), and bar 8 for cadence / phrase-role / connect-to tactics (the first half-cadence lands there). Start with "Baseline" to calibrate your ear on the unreharmonized chorale, then pick any tactic to hear how it transforms the spotlight bar.
