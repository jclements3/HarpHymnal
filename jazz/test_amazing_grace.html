How to audition. Each tactic ships as a 3-bar snippet pair centred on its spotlight bar (bar 5, or bar 8 for cadence / phrase-role / connect-to tactics). Press ▶ A to hear the plain SATB chorale across those three bars; press ▶ B to hear the same three bars with the tactic applied to the middle bar. After B plays, score 0–5: 0 = wrong application / bug-flag, 1 = poor, 2–3 = neutral, 4–5 = drill-worthy. Scores persist locally; use Export scores to save.
